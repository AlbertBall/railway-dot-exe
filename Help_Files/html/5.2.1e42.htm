5.2.2 Service changes

Codes: Sns; Fns.

These always link together such that a 'Sns' service always follows from an earlier 'Fns' service.

Start new service from another service

HH:MM;Sns;other service ref.

Finish and form a new service

HH:MM;Fns;new service ref.

See section 5.11 for some important aspects that will help to avoid errors.
